🎓 Students 🍎 Teachers, TAs & Course Designers

TrailGuide automatically unlocks a set of Teacher Tools once it detects a Teacher or TA enrollment on your connected Canvas account (see how detection works below). Everything on the student features page is available to you too — these are additions on top.

👨‍🏫 Generate Assignment

Draft an assignment based on the content of the page you're viewing, as a starting point to edit and publish yourself.

📋 Generate Rubric

Produces a detailed 4×4 grading rubric (Conceptual Understanding, Application, Technical Accuracy, Documentation) based on your current page's content.

📊 Student Insights

Analyzes the complexity of your course material itself — not your students' individual submissions or grades — to predict the concepts students are most likely to struggle with, and suggests "check for understanding" questions for each.

📅 Lesson Plan

Generates a 50-minute lesson plan following the 5E instructional model (Engage, Explore, Explain, Elaborate, Evaluate) based on your page content.

🔑 Answer Key & Teacher Guide

Surfaces the questions students are most likely to ask about the material, with model answers and common misconceptions.

🧩 Differentiation Strategies

Produces three versions of a learning activity from the same content — one for students needing extra support, a standard version, and an enrichment version for advanced students.

How role detection works Teacher Tools only appear once you've connected Canvas (see Setup) — TrailGuide checks your active Canvas enrollments for a Teacher or TA role. Without a Canvas connection, TrailGuide always defaults to the student view.
What this doesn't do None of these tools pull real student submissions, grades, or gradebook data — every prompt above is built only from the content of the Canvas page you're currently viewing, the same source every other TrailGuide feature uses.
