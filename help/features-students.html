🎓 Students 🍎 Teachers, TAs & Course Designers

Every feature below works on whatever Canvas page (or attached document) you're currently viewing. Trigger any of them from the floating TrailGuide button, the right-click context menu, or the command palette. These are available to everyone — teaching staff get additional tools on their own page.

💬 AI Chat

Ask free-form questions about the course material on your current page and get an answer grounded in that content.

🗂️ Flashcards

Generate a deck of flashcards from a page, an assignment, or an attached document. Flip through them with the Space key while studying.

📚 Summaries & Study Guides

Turn a lecture, reading, or attached PDF/PowerPoint/Word document into a comprehensive summary or a structured study guide.

📝 Practice Quizzes

Generate quiz questions from the current material to test what you actually retained before the real thing.

🎤 Voice Assistant

Toggle hands-free mode with Ctrl+Shift+V to ask questions out loud and hear responses read back to you.

🗺️ Concept Maps

Visualize how the topics and concepts on a page relate to each other, instead of reading them as flat text.

🔍 Deep Dive (Socratic Mode)

Explore a topic in depth without being handed the answer outright — TrailGuide asks guiding questions and breaks complex problems into step-by-step logical hints instead.

Document support TrailGuide automatically detects and reads PDFs, PowerPoints, and Word documents linked on your Canvas pages, so summaries, flashcards, and quizzes can draw on the full document, not just the page text.
